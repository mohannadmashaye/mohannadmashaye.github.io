About me

"Is it even possible to summarize myself in 1 page?" - Mohannad Mashaye

Who am I?

My name is Mohannad Mashaye, and I am a 22-year old Somali-Canadian currently based in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. I have recently graduated with a Bachelor of Computer Science from Carleton University. Thus, I am eager to explore opportunities in front-end development and/or UI design at an exciting company.

What do I do?

I build elegant user interfaces and software applications using multiple programming/markup languages such as: JavaScript, Node.js, ReactJS, HTML5, CSS3, C++, and Python. I love front-end development and user interface design for three reasons. Firstly, I am able to see the code I have written on a web page in real time, which is incredibly encouraging. Secondly, I get to explore my creative side by designing and building beautiful user interfaces. Lastly, I am gifted with the opportunity to work on projects used and seen by users across the globe.

What am I passionate about?

Writing clean code and designing appealing user interfaces (obviously 😉)! However, in my free time I love to read about history, write about various topics, hike, travel, play basketball, surf the web, and spend time with friends and family.
